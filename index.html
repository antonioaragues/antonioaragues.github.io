Antonio Aragüés

Product Leader building software for over 15 years. Generalist, technical, and driven by complex problems.

Hi, I'm Antonio. I'm currently Product Lead at Celonis. I have extensive experience navigating both B2B and B2C landscapes, helping scale companies like Typeform, Eventbrite, and Liferay.

My journey has been hands-on from the start. I've built products from every seat at the table: as a Software Engineer, Engineering Manager, or Founder. I thrive in ambiguity and love simplifying complex technical challenges.

My career has been a steady evolution from building software to shaping product strategy. I started as a Software Engineer and later co-founded an e-Health startup, where I served as CTO and experienced firsthand the realities of taking products from idea to production. That early technical grounding still shapes how I think about product: with deep empathy for engineers and a strong bias toward pragmatic execution.

I eventually transitioned into Product Management, working in high-growth B2B and B2C environments. At Liferay, I helped create a product from scratch, taking it from an early concept to a successful zero-to-one launch. At Eventbrite, I led the Commerce area, owning the purchase experience and order management systems that supported millions of users worldwide.

Later, at Typeform, I focused on helping customers realize value from their data, leading the Insights & Analytics pillar to turn responses into clear, actionable insights. Today at Celonis, I lead ecosystem and third-party product teams, defining platform strategy and enabling developers and partners through APIs, the Marketplace, and extensibility tools that build on the Process Intelligence Platform.

What motivates me most is working where technology and business meet. I enjoy bringing clarity to complex problems and helping teams focus on what truly matters, so they can build simple, well-crafted products that people enjoy using and that create real impact.
