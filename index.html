Antonio Aragüés

Product Leader building software for over 15 years. Generalist, technical, and driven by complex problems.

Hi, I'm Antonio. I'm currently Product Lead at Celonis. I have extensive experience navigating both B2B and B2C landscapes, helping scale companies like Typeform, Eventbrite, and Liferay.

My journey has been hands-on from the start. I've built products from every seat at the table: as a Software Engineer, Engineering Manager, or Founder. I thrive in ambiguity and love simplifying complex technical challenges.

My career has been defined by a constant evolution from deep technical engineering to strategic product leadership. I began my journey in the trenches as a Software Engineer and later as a Co-founder & CTO of a e-Health startup (Goodday). This technical foundation gave me a deep empathy for engineering challenges and a pragmatic approach to building software.

As I pivoted into Product Management, I focused on high-growth B2B/B2C environments. At Liferay, I had the opportunity to build a product from scratch, taking it from a concept to a "Zero to One" launch.

Later, at Eventbrite, I led the Commerce area, overseeing the critical purchase experience and order management infrastructure at a massive global scale. Most recently, at Typeform and Celonis, my focus has shifted to enabling ecosystems and leveraging data to drive user value.

I thrive in environments where technology meets business strategy, helping teams cut through complexity to deliver tools that users genuinely love.
